The purpose of this project is to discover and document bacterial species from belly button species for potential use for the next best taste of Improbable Meat. On this webpage, you can choose a sample from the dropdown menu, then visualize the top bacterial cultures found, the bacteria cultures per sample, and the washing frequency for that subject's belly button. From here, the principle researchers can look at how bacterial cultures differ between samples.
